Lumbini

"Birth place of Gautam Buddha."

Lumbini is a Buddhist pilgrimage site in the Rupandehi District of Province No. 5 in Nepal. Lumbini is the one of the world's most important spiritual sites and attracts Buddhist pilgrims from around the world. You can visit over twenty-five Buddhist monasteries built by diverse countries from Vietnam to France, to study Buddhism.

Lumbini has a number of older temples, including the Mayadevi Temple, and various new temples.

  • Ashoka Piller
  • Lumbini Garden
  • Monasteries
